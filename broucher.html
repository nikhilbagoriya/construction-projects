About Us

Welcome to Grewal Telecom!

Established in 2012, Grewal Telecom, Nizampur is a trusted name in underground infrastructure development. We specialize in the installation of Optical Fiber Cables (OFC), pipelines, and gas pipelines using advanced Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) machines and JCBs.

With over a decade of experience, we pride ourselves on delivering efficient, reliable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients. Our skilled team, modern equipment, and commitment to excellence have made us a preferred partner for infrastructure projects across various industries.

At Grewal Telecom, Nizampur we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility in every project we undertake.

Partner with us for innovative solutions that lay the foundation for your future.

Who We Are

At Grewal Telecom Construction Company Located in Nizampur, Haryana. We pride ourselves on being a trusted name in the telecom infrastructure industry. With years of experience, we specialize in designing, building, and maintaining state-of-the-art telecommunication networks.

Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has helped us become a preferred partner for telecom companies across the region.

We believe in combining advanced technology with a skilled workforce to deliver projects that exceed expectations. Whether it’s upgrading existing networks or constructing new ones, Grewal Telecom Construction Company ensures reliability, efficiency, and long-term value for every client we serve.

Our Services
